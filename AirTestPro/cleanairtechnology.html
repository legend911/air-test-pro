Clean Air Technology

The amount of air purification technologies on the market today have risen dramatically. Each one suggests that it is the next greatest thing, but each technology is most likely only good at one function. The design of a product can lend itself to being an all-purpose product. The design of a products is truly what makes it unique to so many others. Fancy lights and touchscreens are nice, but you want something that will actually work. Many companies are just in the market to make a few extra bucks and truly haven't done the research and development needed to make a revolutionary product. finding out if their products field results are true is hard to do. I will provide you with my own testing results that are independent of any air purifier company on my testing webpage Indoor Air Quality Test.

Before you go any further, let me tell you that an air purification technology is only part of the equation. If it is used in the wrong way or poorly it can be just as bad as anything else. For example, if you put an air purifier in the corner of a room its performance and results will decrease drastically. Most air purifiers are made to gather air from all four sides and sometimes below. If it doesn't have 360 degrees of space to inhale and exhaust air and particulates you will have a marked decrease in performance. Also if the manufacturer decides not to use the correct amount of filter media you will not see the results that you expect. Many companies will just throw in a tiny amount of a technology just to say they have it. When you buy it and expect it to last a certain time you are astonished when it is all used up in a matter of days or weeks. I have seen this all to often with activated carbon filters.

In this loosely regulated marketplace it pays to have products that have been thoroughly tested by independent parties. Many companies will do testing themselves or will even pay to have them done. In both situations they have the opportunity to sway the results or the paid testers feel a sort of loyalty to their customer and will not tell the whole truth if it will not please them. Sometimes leaving certain tests out of the equation are enough to get by on results. Fortunately for you, I will be your own personal tester. I will make sure that you are given the information you need to make a great decision to improve your indoor air quality.
