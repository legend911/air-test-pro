Ionic Filter

You may have run across ionic air filters while looking at TV and seeing a commercial stating how it cleans the air without replaceable filters and while making no noise. Most people look at this and see a win win situation and look into purchasing the product if they have a need for it. Unfortunately, they are given an eye opening experience when they see what it does.... or doesn't do.

What I want to simply say is that ionic technology has many things wrong with it that you should know. First ionic technology created negative ions that can cause a wide variety of issues when they are in high concentration like what can happen in your home. Ozone is the more common name for this collection of negative ions. The metal blades that collect dust can't hold much and when they fill up you will hear a zapping sound that is quite annoying.

How It Works

Ionic technology works by creating negative ions and either releasing them into the air or capturing them internally. When I say they are captured internally it is only percentage of them that are kept in the system and a lot of them leak out. When yuo release negative ions in the air they adhere to particulates and microbials in the air and then they gather weight and fall to the floor. When they are kept in the machine they are created and they adhere to particulates in the machne and the position ions in the metal blades attract them and they stuck there. Unfortunately, these metal blades only have so much surface space and once they runs out it cannot capture anymore.

When negative ions hit the floor they dissipate and all those particulates are just waiting to be disturbed and put right back in the air. It is almost useless to even use it for dust and pollen. It is best used for gas molecules like smoke and heavy smells. The ions created inside of the ionic air purifier stay in there until it gets full. That is when you will start hear zaps and weird popping sounds like you have a bug zapper in your room. Also negative ions will leak out of the device and in small rooms it can easily concentrate and cause health issues for the young and old.
