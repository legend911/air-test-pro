Electrostatic Filter

The use of static electricity in an air filter is not a new concept. If you have an HVAC system installed in your home already then you are using this technology. The air filter that filters the air going back into the HVAC system is an electrostatic air filter. It is a rather thin air filter and needs to use the extra electrostatic charge to attract dust. If you go to a big retail store you will usually find these in the aisle for filter media of all kinds.

You can find electrostatic air filters being used within air purifiers as well. I believe that this is a useful thing, but it usually it just for show. I have grown tired of air purifier companies trying throw every single technology possible into one system and expect them all to work well together. You don't know that this practice is bad because companies just alert you that it uses a technology not how well it works in this current application.

How It Works

Electrostatic filter media works in a very simple way. The filter media is charged with a positive charge and the negative charge on dust particles are attracted to the fibers and get stuck. You see this happen all the time when you look at your dusty tv or when you can't get that dust from sticking to your freshly pressed dress pants. The fact that air is pulled through the filter already is great, but usually these are not thick and it truly needs to be charged to work better. If you look at your HVAC you will notice that plenty of dust still gets through and the device still works, but not as well as it could.

If you own an air conditioner they will tell you to periodically to clean the coils of dust to increase its performance. The same holds true for an HVAC system. The best thing you could do is upgrade the cheap air filter you are using for a True HEPA air filter. You will capture more dust and other particulates and you will make your HVAC last longer with less problems. The best part is with this implementation it will not cost that much more and these air filters are readily available for most sizes.
