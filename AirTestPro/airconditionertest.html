Air Conditioner Test

The testing of air within a room would not be complete if we didn't account of situations that will happen quite often in a lot of homes. A simple test of an empty box will tell you baseline information, which is nice to have, but we live in the real world. Air conditioners send cold air into a room, which is capable of doing several things. First it sends a secondary air circulation to a room that already has an air purifier running. It also sends cooler air that will fall vertically and push up the hotter air faster. When cold and hot air hit it other you get wind from the rapid rising and falling of air.

This test can answer a question many people have thought about when having multiple fan based machines in their rooms. Air is moving, but is it helping or hurting air purification. Even if you don't have an air purifier....what does it do to particulates. You will probably notice that dust spend a bit longer in the suspended state if you have a fan with no air purifier. I will show you how all this air movement effects particulates and your air purification. This will help you decide what you do when you come across these situations.
