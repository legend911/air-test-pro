Ultraviolet Filter

Ultraviolet light is a well known for its ability to destroy harmful pathogens (bacteria, viruses,etc...). Hospitals use UV filters through their HVAC systems to continually stop these pathogens from spreading. This is in addition to the quarantine rooms they create for deadly viruses like the ones you have seen in the movies. Fortunately, your home is not that bad and their is a possibility that UV filters could help you somewhat.

I will say that you shouldn't put all your hopes and dreams into this technology. You don't have to make direct contact with anything like antibacterial filters, but they have to be in the light for a certain period of time. Because of this period of time their is a percentage of pathogens that will make it through the filter unscathed. To me that is unacceptable, but for you it may be acceptable. This percentage is variable and depends on certain environmental variables. I would just temper your thoughts on what the side of the air purifier box states it does and what it actually is capable of doing.

How It Works

The process is extremely simple for UV filters. The ultraviolet light is capable of destroying most harmful pathogens that are within your home. If you allow the UV light enough time it can destroy them 100% of the time. The time depends on what needs to be destroyed, but it could be instant to several seconds. Many systems believe the optimal way to destroy these pahtogens is to have them make multiple passes through the UV filter. I believe this is a good thing to do as well, but it adds to the run time of the air purification system.

I believe if you have a real need to kill bacteria and viruses floating around in your home the addition of a UV filter can truly help you. You will have to run it for an extended time to destroy 90%+ of them. For people who have medial diseases or have small children it can be a great addition to one of the technologies with your air purifier. A good thing about it is if this filter is the only thing you want you don't have to get a super expensive system you can get something that is suitable for your needs without all the extras.
