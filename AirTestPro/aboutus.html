About the Owner

My name is Dwight Norris and I am the owner of Air Test Pro. If you are not suffering from respiratory issues yourself, you probably know somebody that does and would like to help them. I have had issues most of my life. At birth I was regulated in a bubble that covered my crib because I unable to breathe regular air for over 2 weeks. Now I am a year long allergy sufferer and I am determined to help you find a way to breathe easier.

Currently, I am working on becoming an Air Quality Specialist. I have been working as a Quality Engineer for software and hardware companies for over 8 years. I am the kind of person that must make sure the product is of the highest quality and workmanship. I have structured my testing to hit all the key features of each product to see the performance visually through graphical data.

Quality Assurance

I have spent a large quantity of my career in the field of Quality Assurance. Many of these skills can be used to help me research, test and display results for air quality. I am going to use my skills to do just that on this website. Quality asssurance requires that you know the processes involved in a product whether it be software or hardware. You also you have to be thorough at testing so that you may provide the customer with the best possible product. I believe these things are important and they will help me help you find exactly what you need.

Within many of my webpages you will see pictures and videos of the actual technologies and products that I am talking about. I have gone through this amount of work to actually show that I am actually testing these products and you can watch me do it. When I describe a technology you will see it up front and center. You don't have to rely on data on the side of a box or what some other website copied from the manufacturer. The data I am gathering is real and is not bias in any way. I do not take money from companies to do tests and reviews. You can rest assured that the information you are seeing is done by a real person in an environment where people actually live and breathe.

You may also see that I have graphical data and a web application within my webpages. The graphical data is there to help you understand how all the variables that we test compare in different situations. Everybody's issues are not always the same. One allergy sufferer is not the same as the next. One HEPA filter does not perform the same as another. I intended to collect this data and display it in a fashion that is usable for you to discover what works or why a product you have is not performing how you would expect it. Many people have a certain view of how bad their air quality is or how well an air purifier works and it may just turn out not to be the case.
